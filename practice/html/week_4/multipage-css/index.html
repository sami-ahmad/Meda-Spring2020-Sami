Welcome

Treehouse is an online learning platform that teaches coding and web development skills, such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and PHP. You must have an active San Francisco Public Library account to access Treehouse. To create or reactivate your Treehouse account, submit this form. Once your request is processed, Treehouse will send you an email confirming your account. Please be patient as account creation may take a few days. Library account holders may only have ONE account with Treehouse.
